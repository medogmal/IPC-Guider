# Privacy Policy for IPC Guider

**Last Updated:** January 2025

## Overview

IPC Guider ("the App") is an offline-first mobile application designed for Infection Prevention and Control (IPC) professionals. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data Collection

**IPC Guider is designed with privacy as a core principle:**

- ✅ **100% Offline Operation** - The app operates entirely offline with no internet connection required
- ✅ **No Personal Data Collection** - We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information
- ✅ **No User Accounts** - No registration, login, or user accounts required
- ✅ **No Analytics or Tracking** - We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising services
- ✅ **No Third-Party Services** - No data is shared with third parties

## Local Data Storage

The app stores data locally on your device only:

### What is Stored Locally:
- **Calculator Results** - Your calculation history and saved results
- **User Preferences** - App settings and theme preferences
- **Quiz Progress** - Your quiz scores and progress (if applicable)
- **Notes and Tags** - Any notes or tags you add to saved entries

### Important Notes:
- All data is stored **only on your device**
- Data is **never transmitted** to external servers
- You can **delete all data** at any time through the app settings
- Uninstalling the app will **permanently delete** all local data

## Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

### Storage Permission
- **Purpose:** To export your calculator results and history to Excel/CSV files
- **Usage:** Only used when you explicitly choose to export data
- **Data Access:** Only accesses files you create through the app

### No Other Permissions Required
- No camera access
- No location access
- No contacts access
- No microphone access
- No network/internet access required

## Data Security

- All data is stored locally using industry-standard encryption (Hive database)
- No data transmission means no risk of data interception
- You maintain complete control over your data

## Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time:

1. **Individual Entries:** Delete specific calculator results or history entries within the app
2. **All Data:** Clear all app data through:
   - App Settings → Clear All Data
   - Device Settings → Apps → IPC Guider → Clear Data
3. **Complete Removal:** Uninstall the app to permanently remove all data

## Children's Privacy

IPC Guider is designed for healthcare professionals and is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
