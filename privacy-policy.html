IPC Guider - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 15, 2025

Summary: IPC Guider is a 100% offline application. We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All your data remains on your device.

1. Information Collection and Use

IPC Guider is designed with privacy as a core principle. The app operates entirely offline and does not collect any personal information.

What We DO NOT Collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or statistics
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data
  • Patient health information (PHI)
  • Any data transmitted to external servers

2. Local Data Storage

All data generated by the app is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms:

Data Stored Locally:

  • Calculation Results: Saved using Hive database (encrypted local storage)
  • User Settings: Preferences saved using SharedPreferences (local storage)
  • Calculation History: Historical records stored locally for your reference
  • Exported Files: Excel, CSV, PDF, and PNG files saved to your device's storage

Data Control:

  • You have complete control over your data
  • You can delete individual calculation records at any time
  • You can clear all app data through device settings
  • Uninstalling the app removes all local data

3. Permissions

IPC Guider requires minimal permissions to function:

Android Permissions:

  • Storage Access: Required for exporting calculation results to Excel, CSV, PDF, and PNG files
  • No Internet Permission: The app does not require or use internet connectivity

iOS Permissions:

  • Photo Library Access: Required only when you choose to save exported images
  • Files Access: Required for exporting documents

4. Third-Party Services

IPC Guider does NOT use any third-party services, including:

  • No analytics services (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase Analytics)
  • No advertising networks
  • No crash reporting services
  • No cloud storage or backup services
  • No social media integrations

5. External Links

The app contains reference links to external resources from reputable organizations:

  • World Health Organization (WHO)
  • Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC)
  • Association for Professionals in Infection Control and Epidemiology (APIC)
  • Gulf Cooperation Council Directorate of Infection Prevention and Control (GDIPC)
  • Clinical and Laboratory Standards Institute (CLSI)
  • Infectious Diseases Society of America (IDSA)

These links open in your device's web browser and are subject to those organizations' privacy policies. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites.

6. Data Security

We take data security seriously:

  • Local Encryption: Sensitive data is stored using Hive's encrypted storage
  • No Network Transmission: No data is transmitted over the internet
  • Secure Coding Practices: The app follows Flutter security best practices
  • Regular Updates: We maintain the app with security patches and updates

7. Protected Health Information (PHI)

IPC Guider is designed for infection prevention calculations and does NOT require or collect Protected Health Information (PHI):

  • The app uses aggregate data (e.g., number of infections, patient-days)
  • No patient names, medical record numbers, or identifiable information is required
  • Users are responsible for ensuring they do not enter PHI into the app
  • If PHI is accidentally entered, it remains on your device and can be deleted
Important: The app includes an optional "Include PHI" toggle in settings, which is OFF by default. This setting only affects export file naming and does not change data collection practices.

8. Children's Privacy

IPC Guider is a professional medical reference tool intended for healthcare professionals. The app is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Medical Disclaimer

IPC Guider is for educational and professional reference purposes only:

  • The app provides calculation tools and reference information
  • It is NOT a substitute for professional medical judgment
  • Always follow your institution's policies and protocols
  • Consult with qualified healthcare professionals for clinical decisions
  • The developer assumes no liability for clinical decisions made using this app

10. International Users

IPC Guider can be used worldwide. Since all data is stored locally on your device, no data crosses international borders through our app.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in:

  • App functionality
  • Legal requirements
  • Privacy best practices

Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically.

12. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • Right to Access: All your data is accessible within the app
  • Right to Delete: You can delete any or all data at any time
  • Right to Export: You can export your data to Excel, CSV, PDF, or PNG
  • Right to Portability: Exported files can be used in other applications

13. Compliance

IPC Guider's privacy-by-design approach aligns with major privacy regulations:

  • GDPR (EU): No personal data processing
  • CCPA (California): No personal information sale or sharing
  • HIPAA (US Healthcare): No PHI collection or transmission
